Call for Special Sessions Proposals

The Organizing Committee invites prospective organizers to submit proposals for the DSP2015 special sessions. The special sessions aim to complement the regular sessions with specialized, advanced or important research directions that are of interest to DSP conference participants. Each special session will have five presentations. The special session organizer shall invite the papers, arrange for the review, and decide on the acceptance.

Submission Details

Each special session proposal shall include the following:

1. Title of the special session

2. A short write-up on the theme of the proposed special session

3. Short biography of the session organizer

4. If possible, a list of titles and authors of prospective papers

Schedule of Important Dates

Submission of special session proposals : Feb 9, 2015
Submission of special session papers : March 2, 2015 March 16, 2015
Submission of special session camera ready papers : May 4, 2015
Notification of acceptance : April 13, 2015

Proposals for special sessions should be submitted to the DSP2015 Special Session Chair, Dr. Lee Jun Wei (jwlee@ntu.edu.sg).
